Who are we?

We are a pro-life organization located in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada.

What do we do?

We advocate for the legal rights of the unborn through spreading information and collecting petitions. Currently our focus is getting bill C-233 passed.

What is C-233?

C-233 is a bill proposed by MP Cathay Wagantall which would make sex-selective abortion illegal in all provinces of Canada.
